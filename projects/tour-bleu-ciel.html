Tour Bleu Ciel

LocationHarwood Village, Dallas, USA
Scale42,800 m² · 114 m · 32 storeys
Units158 luxury apartments · 7,500 m² of terraces
OfficeWilmotte & Associés
ClientHarwood International ProgrammeLuxury residential tower SignatureCascading planted terraces

A Parisian sensibility in downtown Dallas.

Rising 114 metres above Harwood Village, Tour Bleu Ciel stacks 158 high-specification residences across 32 floors and wraps them in 7,500 m² of planted, outdoor terraces. The tower brings a European language of setbacks and loggias to a Texas skyline defined by flat, sealed glass curtain walls.

Role

Nicolas worked on the tower as a senior designer in the Paris studio, with input on façade modulation, unit mix studies and the coordination of the planted terraces as architectural — rather than purely landscape — elements.
