SMABTP Headquarters & Hotel

LocationParis, France
Scale34,000 m² offices · 5,000 m² hotel
StatusCompetition winner
SustainabilityHQE · BREEAM Very Good · LEED Gold
OfficeWilmotte & Associés
ClientSMABTP (Société Mutuelle d'Assurance du Bâtiment et des Travaux Publics) ProgrammeCorporate headquarters · Hotel PhasesAPS / APD / Permis de construire

A headquarters for the French construction sector.

The new headquarters for SMABTP brings together 34,000 m² of workplace with a 5,000 m² hotel, won in competition and pursued through to planning permission. The building is designed to triple certification — HQE, BREEAM Very Good and LEED Gold — an unusual commitment in the Paris office market at the time.

Role

Nicolas was senior project architect on the delivery team, working from schematic design through design development and the French permis de construire package, with responsibilities spanning façade, plan and environmental strategy.

Sustainability as form

Passive strategies — orientation, depth of plan, solar shading — set the envelope geometry rather than following it. The active systems then tune a building that was designed to breathe well from the outset.
