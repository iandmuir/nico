MARS Inc. Global Headquarters

LocationMcLean, Virginia
ProgrammeCorporate headquarters campus
PhasesSD / DD / CD / CA
OfficeShalom Baranes Associates
ClientMars, Incorporated TypeWorkplace / HQ DeliveryFull SD through Construction Administration

A headquarters for a family-owned global business.

The global headquarters relocates one of the world's largest privately-held companies to a single campus in McLean, consolidating leadership, research and associate functions under one identity. The brief was driven as much by values as by square footage — transparency, flexibility and a workplace that reflects the Mars Five Principles.

Role

Nicolas contributed across all four delivery phases — schematic design, design development, construction documentation and construction administration — working on the core envelope and interior coordination with a specialist workplace team.

Design intent

The architecture privileges daylight and long views through a reserved, precisely detailed exterior. Interior volumes are organised around collaboration space and a central atrium, with generous, flexible floor plates for a workforce that operates across dozens of product categories.
