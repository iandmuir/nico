A building for the Scuderia.

At the heart of the Maranello campus, the Sport Management Centre houses the operational nerve-system of the Scuderia Ferrari F1 team — race strategy, engineering, driver training, media and hospitality — under a single roof immediately adjacent to Ferrari's historic production facilities.

Delivery across jurisdictions

The project was delivered by a French design office under Italian regulatory procedures, moving through APS, APD and DCE in the French model while being documented in Italian definitivo and esecutivo formats. Nicolas led the design-development team and tender package coordination.

Architectural character

The architecture is restrained and technical — a fitting counterpart to the engineering culture of its occupant — with a precision-detailed façade system, long horizontal lines, and carefully tuned interior environments for high-performance work.
