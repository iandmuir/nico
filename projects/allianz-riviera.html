A stadium wrapped in light.

Won as a public-private-partnership competition, the Allianz Riviera is a 35,000-seat multi-purpose stadium on the Plaine du Var. Its signature is a translucent ETFE envelope draped over a hybrid roof structure — steel trusses laced with glue-laminated timber ribs — that lifts the building's apparent weight and softens its impact on the surrounding landscape.

Role

Nicolas worked on the project from competition through construction, with lead responsibility for the envelope — roof geometry, façade detailing, and the ETFE cushion system — and for the hybrid structural coordination between steel and timber.

Sustainability

The stadium was developed to French HQE standards, with rainwater harvesting, geothermal cooling, and photovoltaic surfaces integrated into the roof — all expressed as architecture rather than bolted on.
