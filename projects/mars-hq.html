A headquarters shaped by values, not just brief.

The global headquarters relocates one of the world's largest privately-held companies to a consolidated campus in McLean — bringing together leadership, research and associate functions under one identity. The brief was driven as much by the Mars Five Principles as by programme: transparency, flexibility, and a workplace that reflects a deeply human corporate culture.

Role

Nicolas contributed across all four delivery phases — schematic design, design development, construction documentation and construction administration — working on the core envelope and interior coordination with a specialist workplace team.

Design Intent

The architecture privileges daylight and long views through a reserved, precisely detailed exterior. Interior volumes are organised around collaboration space and a central atrium, with generous floor plates for a workforce that operates across dozens of product categories globally.
