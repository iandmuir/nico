A headquarters for the French construction sector.

The new headquarters for SMABTP — the mutual insurance body for the French construction industry — brings 34,000 m² of workplace together with a 5,000 m² hotel, won in competition and developed through to planning permission. The building is designed to triple certification: HQE, BREEAM Very Good and LEED Gold — an unusual commitment in the Paris office market at the time of design.

Role

Nicolas was senior project architect on the delivery team, with responsibilities spanning façade, planning and environmental strategy — from schematic design through design development and the French permis de construire package.

Sustainability as Form

Passive strategies — orientation, depth of plan, solar shading geometry — set the envelope form rather than following it. Active systems then tune a building that was designed to breathe well from its inception.
