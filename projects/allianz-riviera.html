A stadium wrapped in light.

Won as a public-private-partnership competition, the Allianz Riviera is a 35,000-seat multi-purpose stadium on the Plaine du Var. Its signature is a translucent ETFE envelope draped over a hybrid roof structure — steel trusses laced with glue-laminated timber ribs — that lifts the building's apparent weight and softens its impact on the landscape.

Envelope Leadership

Nicolas worked on the project from competition through construction, with lead responsibility for the envelope — roof geometry, façade detailing, and the ETFE cushion system — and for the hybrid structural coordination between the steel and timber elements.

Sustainability

The stadium was designed to French HQE standards with rainwater harvesting, geothermal cooling and photovoltaic surfaces integrated into the roof — expressed as architecture, not added as afterthought.
